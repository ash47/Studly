About Studly

Student life is filled with commitments and responsibilities that make time management an essential skill in order to succeed.

Struggling undergraduates often have multiple mechanisms in place just to keep track of their separate university requirements. This can include calendar apps, notebooks, post-it-notes, budget records, document folders and sprawling Excel documents, to name a few.

Studly is a web application for students of The University of Melbourne, targeted at mobile platforms that will make lecture attendance and note-taking an absolute breeze. The app is accessible from your smartphone, presenting a timetable of your lectures and tutorials for the semester and records which of them you have attended and taken notes for.

Keeping track of lectures has never been this easy.
